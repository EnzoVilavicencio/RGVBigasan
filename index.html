🚀 How to Deploy This POS Online (Free)

⚠️ Important: This POS saves data in your browser's local storage. Data stays on the device and browser you use. Export CSV regularly to back up your data!

Option A: GitHub Pages (Recommended — Free Forever)
1
Go to github.com and create a free account.
2
Click New Repository. Name it tindahan-pos. Set it to Public. Click Create.
3
Save this webpage as index.html on your computer (File → Save Page As in your browser).
4
In your GitHub repo, click Add file → Upload files. Upload index.html. Click Commit.
5
Go to Settings → Pages. Under "Source", select main branch. Click Save.
6
Your POS will be live at: https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/tindahan-pos 🎉

Option B: Netlify Drop (Fastest — 30 seconds)
1
Save this webpage as index.html on your computer.
2
Go to netlify.com/drop — no account needed!
3
Drag and drop your index.html file. Done! You get a free URL instantly.

💡 Tips
• Use Export CSV regularly to back up your data.
• Best used on a tablet or laptop at the counter.
• Bookmark the deployed URL on your device for quick access.
• Clearing browser data will erase stored info — Export CSV often!
