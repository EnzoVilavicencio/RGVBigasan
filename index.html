🚀 How to Deploy This POS Online (Free)

⚠️ Important: This POS saves data in your browser's local storage. Data stays on the device and browser you use. Export CSV regularly to back up your data!

Option A: GitHub Pages (Recommended — Free Forever)
1
Go to github.com and create a free account.
2
Click New Repository. Name it tindahan-pos. Set to Public. Click Create.
3
Save this webpage as index.html (File → Save Page As in your browser).
4
In your repo, click Add file → Upload files. Upload index.html. Click Commit.
5
Go to Settings → Pages. Under "Source", select main branch. Click Save.
6
Your POS is live at: https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/tindahan-pos 🎉

Option B: Netlify Drop (Fastest — 30 seconds, no account needed)
1
Save this webpage as index.html.
2
Go to netlify.com/drop.
3
Drag and drop your index.html file. Done! You get a free URL instantly.

💡 Tips
• Use Export CSV regularly to back up your data.
• Best used on a tablet or laptop at the counter.
• Clearing browser cache/data will erase stored info — Export CSV often!
